Version 4.0 - The game saviour update

Version 4.0 is the game saviour update. The Game will automatically saved to you device name!
This update will load the save when you start playing and then save it through out playing. No Sign up or interaction needed!
A new side bar was added to make the buttons at the bottom of the screen more accessible and less scrolling is required. (Implemented from Beta)
A sell share rent button was added to so you would sell the least amount of shares to pay for your rent. (Implemented from Beta)
When hovering over a button it will now change colour. (Implemented from Beta)
The buy/sell x10 was removed and replaced with a buy/sell 50%. (Implemented from Beta)
