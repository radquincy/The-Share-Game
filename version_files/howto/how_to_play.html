How to play

Press the "start" button to start the game.
Once the game has started you have to buy and sell shares to make profit and pay for your rent.
If you don't pay for your rent in time you get kicked out of your apartment and lose the game.
You can buy and sell the shares for the same price each "turn" or until the end of the day.
Each Day or "turn [next day]" changes the price of the shares to make you lose or gain money.
The Game is strategy and luck it may take a while to progress far in the game.
